Espresso Martini

Smooth, bold, and irresistibly chic. The espresso martini is where coffee meets cocktail culture. Born in the 1980s, this modern classic delivers the perfect balance of rich espresso, velvety liqueur, and a hint of sweetness!

Ingredients

  • 1½ oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Baileys Irish Cream
  • 1 oz Freshly brewed espresso
  • ½ oz Coffee liqueur
  • Ice cubes
  • Coffee beans or cocoa powder (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Brew espresso – Make a fresh shot and let it cool slightly (still warm is best for foam).
  2. Fill shaker –Add ice cubes to a cocktail shaker.
  3. Combine –Pour in vodka, Baileys, coffee liqueur, and espresso.
  4. Shake vigorously for about 15–20 seconds until the mixture is chilled and frothy.
  5. Strain and serve –Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  6. Garnish –Top with 3 coffee beans or a light dusting of cocoa powder for a velvety finish.

Sip, Savor, Enjoy

That’s it –your espresso martini is ready to work its magic. It’s bold, creamy, and just the right amount of boozy buzz. Perfect for late-night laughs, after-dinner hangs, or when you just want coffee and cocktails in one glass. Go ahead, shake it up and sip happy!
